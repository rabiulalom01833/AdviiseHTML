About Adviise

About Adviise

Our team and board of directors are innovators with experience ranging from gaming and academia to sports and media. If we could disrupt other industries, why not healthcare?

“Healthcare has become a frustrating and opaque experience for both consumers and providers.”

Alexi Alizadeh

CEO & Founder of Adviise

When we began this journey, the first thing that became evident was that the serious lack of transparency in the healthcare process was detrimental to both consumers and providers. And Adviise was born.

The Adviise Solution

  • Accountability
  • Efficiency
  • Transparency

Accountability

Accountability through a review system you can trust. The customer reviews you can find on Adviise are from real people, so you can rest assured that they are both accurate and traceable. We use each physician’s National Provider Identifier (NPI) number, rather than just their physical address, so we can ensure the highest levels of accountability and that the reviews posted are provider-specific.

As Adviise continues to aggregate more medical providers on our platform the database will grow and the platform will offer:

  • The ability to collect payments
  • Standardized digital registration forms
  • HIPAA-compliant telemedicine appointments
  • Secure automated fulfillment of medical record requests

    • Efficiency

    Efficiency with Real Time Automated Appointments.

    “Patients often have a long back and forth with their doctors’ offices – and that’s just to get an initial appointment scheduled.”

    Alexi Alizadeh

    We provide a secure and fast way to book appointments just as easily as consumers are used to when purchasing air travel, making restaurant reservations or booking hotel rooms. In addition, Adviise provides automated reminders and the ability to seamlessly make follow-up appointments.

    Transparency

    The Adviise solution allows patients to compare services, providers, and healthcare costs in one single platform. Our powerful search platform will become an invaluable tool for finding the right physicians, healthcare practitioners or clinical practices.
